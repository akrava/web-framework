Third party modules

There are some classes, that use third part libraries:

  • JsonMiddleware - nlohmann_json
  • HtmlMiddleware - mustache

This modules are intended to simplify the creation of web applications. Both libraries are licensed under the MIT License.

The above listed modules are not important in framework's engine, so you can remove them at all. You can use another modules.
